Privacy Policy

Effective: January 1, 2026 Last updated: January 1, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how GoodTek LLC ("GoodTek," "we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and shares information when you visit our website at goodtek.co (the "Site") or use any application, product, or service we provide (collectively, the "Services"). We are a Wyoming limited liability company and we operate our Services in the United States.

We believe privacy is the default, not a feature. We collect the minimum information needed to operate and improve our Services, and we will tell you plainly what we collect and why.

01Information we collect

Information you provide

When you contact us by email, sign up for an account, request support, or otherwise communicate with us, you may provide information such as your name, email address, and the contents of your message. We use this information to respond to you and to provide the Services you request.

Information collected automatically

When you visit our Site, we may automatically collect limited technical information, including:

  • IP address and approximate, country-level location;
  • Browser type, operating system, and device characteristics;
  • Pages viewed, referring URL, and time spent on the Site;
  • Diagnostic information such as crash reports and basic performance metrics.

We use this information to operate, secure, and improve the Site. We do not use it to build advertising profiles, and we do not sell it.

Information collected through our applications

Our applications are designed to be local-first and privacy-preserving. Where an application processes content on your device, that content stays on your device unless you explicitly choose to sync, share, or back it up. Any data sent to our servers is described in the application's in-app privacy disclosures and on its App Store privacy label.

Some applications use voice or audio input. Audio is processed only for the feature you have invoked (for example, transcription or a voice command), and is not used for advertising, sold to third parties, or used to train third-party models without your explicit consent.

We use information to:

We do not sell personal information. We share information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service providers. We use a small number of vendors (for example, hosting, error reporting, and email delivery providers) who process information on our behalf under written agreements that limit their use of that information.
  • Legal requirements. We may disclose information if required by law, subpoena, or other valid legal process, or if we believe in good faith that disclosure is necessary to protect our rights, your safety, or the safety of others.
  • Business transfers. If GoodTek is involved in a merger, acquisition, financing, or sale of assets, information may be transferred as part of that transaction, subject to this Privacy Policy.

04Data retention

We retain information only for as long as necessary to provide our Services, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements. When information is no longer needed, we delete or anonymize it.

We use industry-standard administrative, technical, and physical safeguards designed to protect information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction. We use encryption in transit and at rest where appropriate. No method of transmission or storage is perfectly secure, but we work to follow current best practices.
